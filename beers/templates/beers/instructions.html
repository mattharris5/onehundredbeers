Contest Instructions

Pay Your Dues

To be formally entered in the contest you need to send Eileen $10 for our joint Kiva fund. You can use PayPal or slip her some cash. If neither of those works, email her and you’ll figure it out.

Register at Untappd

We are using Untappd as our main logging platform. Please register for an account if you don’t already have one. We will be making your usernames available to all the competitors (hey, we’re all in this together), so you can follow along in Untappd or on our website.

During registration, you'll need to include your Untappd RSS feed - that's what will let the system find out what beers you've been drinking. To find it, log in on your computer, under account scroll all the way down to find the RSS link. Copy and paste that into your registration.

Drink, Document, and Win!

You’ve got the list, now get out there and find them! As you imbibe, snap a picture or two for evidence. Evidence includes, but is not limited to, tap handles, menus, labels, receipts, etc. Untappd lets you upload one pic to each check-in - feel free to get creative! We will be pulling the Untappd data into the 100 Beers and presenting a leaderboard and other things. Those features will roll out soon and I’ll let you know as the site gets fancier and more fun. We all have until the bitter end of 2016 to get after these brews.
