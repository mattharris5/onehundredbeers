The premise is simple. There's a list of beers. Drink all of them in a pre-set amount of time. Whoever finishes the list first or gets most of them done the earliest wins.

We use Untappd to track who's had what beer and we encourage rating and reviewing. When you log in for the first time, you'll connect your Untappd account so that every check-in counts!

Get an Untapped account if you don't have one. It's easy and fun! Use the Untappd app on your Android or iOS device, or just sign up on the web.
